---
title: Transitions
description: Scene transitions built as presentations for TransitionSeries
---

Every transition here is a presentation for `@remotion/transitions` — pass it to a `TransitionSeries.Transition` between two sequences and control the pace with `linearTiming` or `springTiming`.
