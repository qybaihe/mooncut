# Design philosophy — Apple discipline, Onda surface

Onda follows **Apple's design discipline** without copying Apple's look.

## The discipline

- **Reduction.** Say less. One idea per section, one focal element per moment. Subtract until only the essential remains.
- **Deference.** The design gets out of the way so the motion is the hero. Chrome is quiet; content is loud.
- **Clarity.** Generous negative space, confident typography, nothing decorative for its own sake.
- **Purposeful motion.** Every animation means something. No gratuitous movement.

## The surface

The surface is deliberately **not** Apple: a dark, premium canvas with a single signature accent and characterful type. Apple's *restraint* + a premium-dark-startup *skin* (closer to Linear / Vercel / Viktor).

A literal Apple clone would be light, neutral, and generic — which fights our goal of being unmistakable. The dusty rose accent (`--onda-accent: #D96B82`) is deliberately ours. Almost every dev tool goes blue or violet; we don't.
